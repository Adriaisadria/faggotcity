Record

I sincerely convinced that my educational background and professional experiences uniquely qualify me as an exceptional candidate for the position of 7th District Keeper of Records and Seal. My education in finance and my experiences in higher education administration as a college Bursar provide the necessary skill set to lead the charge in enhancing our record keeping protocols that are currently in place for the 7th District of the Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc.
For more than 20 years I’ve had professional opportunities to develop and administer effective change, which have included but are not limited to notable chapter operating procedures throughout the state of Mississippi, implementation of strategic initiatives and managed committee chairman to achieve the State of Mississippi organizational goals and objectives. Currently, my responsibilities as a college bursar offer additional significant qualifications for the position of Keeper of Records and Seal to the Mighty 7th District.

Additionally, my vast knowledge of fraternity policy and procedure, excellent communication skills, as well as my ability to accurately report organizational proceedings in a timely and effective manner, have prepared me to serve in such a demanding office of the 7th District Keeper of Records and Seal, which is often referred to as the “BACKBONE” of the fraternity.
